Build Status & Design Overview

Our solar vehicle is actively under development. Rather than publishing placeholder specifications, we focus on sharing our current progress and design direction.

Current Status

  • Phase: Design & prototyping
  • Primary focus: electrical architecture, chassis layout, system integration
  • Near-term goals: rolling chassis and initial testing

Design Priorities

  • Energy efficiency and endurance performance
  • Reliability and serviceability
  • Safety-first electrical and mechanical design

Core Systems

Solar Array
High-efficiency cells with emphasis on power tracking and durability.
Battery System
Energy storage, protection, and monitoring designed for long-distance racing.
Motor & Controller
Electric drivetrain optimized for smooth, efficient operation.
Telemetry
Real-time data collection for diagnostics, strategy, and performance analysis.
Chassis & Aerodynamics
Lightweight structure with an efficiency-driven aerodynamic profile.

Detailed specifications will be published once systems are validated.
