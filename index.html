Design. Build. Race.

Grand Valley Solar Racing is a student-led project at Grand Valley State University focused on designing and racing efficient solar-powered vehicles. We teach engineering, teamwork, and sustainable mobility while competing in regional and national solar challenges.

About Us

We are a multidisciplinary student team that brings together mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering to create a competitive solar vehicle. Our mission is to educate, innovate, and promote renewable transportation.
