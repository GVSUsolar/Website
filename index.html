About Us

GV Solar Racing is a student organization at Grand Valley State University founded in 2017. We strive to be not only future engineers but also leaders in technological advancement. Our mission is to design and build GVSU’s first solar-powered race car and compete in the American Solar Challenge, a multi-day 1500–2000 mile endurance race for solar vehicles. Students from all majors are welcome—no prior experience required.

Build Status & Design Overview

Our vehicle is actively under development. Rather than publishing placeholder specifications, we focus on sharing our current progress and design direction.

Current Phase

  • Stage: Design & prototyping
  • Focus: electrical architecture, chassis layout, subsystem integration
  • Short-term goals: rolling chassis and initial system testing

Design Priorities

  • Energy efficiency and endurance performance
  • Serviceable and reliable systems
  • Safety-driven electrical and mechanical design

Core Systems in Development

Solar Array
High-efficiency solar cells with power tracking and durability as primary goals.
Battery System
Energy storage, protection, and monitoring designed for long-distance racing.
Motor & Controller
Electric drivetrain optimized for smooth control and efficiency.
Telemetry
Real-time data collection for strategy, diagnostics, and performance analysis.
Chassis & Aerodynamics
Lightweight structure and aero design focused on minimizing drag.

Specifications will be published once systems are finalized and validated.
