About Us

GV Solar Racing is a student organization at Grand Valley State University founded in 2017. We strive to be not only future engineers but also leaders in technological advances. We have been diligently working on the design and construction of Grand Valley’s first solar-powered race car. Our goal is to establish a club that appreciates and embraces alternative energy improvements in the automotive industry and successfully compete in the American Solar Challenge, where teams from around the globe construct a solar-powered vehicle to take on a multi-day 1500-2000-mile cross-country course.
